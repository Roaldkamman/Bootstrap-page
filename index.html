About Bootstrap

The beautiful efficiency of using bootstrap can bring more speed to the development process.

That's why I decided to make a quick project and add it to my portfolio. It's a great piece of technology. A tool I will use when we need to get stuff done fast.

Experts use Bootstrap

Everyone starts their projects up using bootstrap. You can always optimize the CSS later. It's more important to get your product out the door.

Minimal viable products are easily created using Bootstrap. Ensuring maximum speed with minimum failures, bugs and other issues.

Bootstrap Skills

  • JQuery: Plugins that enhance Bootstrap's range.
  • CSS: For doing the few things you can't easily do with Bootstrap.
  • HTML: The basics.
  • DESIGN: You still need a sense of design and layout.
  • UX: Know what your users want and what their behavior on your website is going to be.
